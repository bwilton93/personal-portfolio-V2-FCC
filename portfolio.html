About Me

My name is Barney, and I try to make cool stuff on the internet. I live in the United Kingdom and currently work as a Data Analyst for a web design agency. Although this is not my passion. After working on a project using Python in February 2020 I knew I wanted to persue a career in software development. The feeling of joy you get when solve that one problem that eludes you is unrivalled.

Although I wish to become a Software Engineer my life is not entirely ruled by computers. In my spare time I enjoy sports, playing hockey for a London team every Saturday across the winter. My other, considerably more nerdy, hobby is Dungeons & Dragons. Meeting every other week with a group of friends to kick back, relax, have some beers and save the world from impending doom.
